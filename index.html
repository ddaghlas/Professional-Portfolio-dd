About Me

Hello,
I'm Diana Daghlas, a certified Full-Stack Web Developer based in the MetroDetroit area of Michigan. I am certified through Michigan State University's College of Engineering, having graduated from their accelerated Full-Stack program on September 18, 2021. I also have a BA in English and Journalism from Wayne State University, however, I have always had a passion for Computer Science.

After spending 10 years in the Food and Beverage industry as a Sommelier, both consulting on, and distributing wine, I decided to take my "hobby" and turn it into a career.

Throughout the duration of the Full-Stack program, I am now able to improve and optimize an applications functionality and styling. Equipped with thorough knowledge of JavaScript, jQuery, CSS, Bootstrap, HTML, Node.js, Express.js, MySQL, MongoDB, Mongoose, React.js and React-Bootstrap, I am determined to become one of the best in the field. As a person who plans on being a life-long Academic, web development provides the medium for continuous learning.
